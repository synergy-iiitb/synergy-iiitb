About Technik 2021

IIIT Bangalore's student culture has grown significantly over the years. With Infin8, Spandan, Yamini, Sangam, Umang etc. taking their extravagant current form, but we never had a student led Tech-fest in our campus. This is the first attempt to change that and we hope to start small and scale just like the others. Technik is the first of a kind student organised Tech-fest of IIIT Bangalore aiming to showcase some of the amazing work the students have been doing and also provide them with an opportunity to explore and expand their horizons in this vast world of Technology. The aim is to allow students to interact with seniors, corpporates, professors in a fashion that inspires them to take up challenges and give a platform for them to innovate.
